Animatronic Prosthetic Hand A BCE Arduino Project.

A project using Arduino to control a robotic hand though a bluetooth connection with an Android or iOS device. Designed with amputees in mind, with cusomised app interfaces among other features. Designed with both cost and function in mind, and with aesthetics on the top.

Midi Controller An Arduino Midi Contoller Sketch.

Use this sketch to turn your Arduino into a Midi controler. Uses up to 16 buttons and has programable notes and velocitys. Designed to be adapeted to your project or used as referance for working with Arduino and the Midi interface.
