Organized Projects

  • Animatronic Prosthetic Hand
    Brighton College Engineering

    A project using Arduino to control a robotic hand though a Bluetooth connection with an Android or iOS device. Designed with amputees in mind, with cusomised app interfaces among other features. Designed with both cost and function in mind, and with aesthetics on the top.

Game Jams

  • Bacon Game Jam 07

    My entry to Bacon-Game-Jam-07, it's a Super Hexagon remake (a super easy one at that). It still has bugs, I might fix them at some point, but I like that this was all made in 48hrs. I cam 32nd out of 252 paricipants.

  • Bacon Game Jam 08

    My entry to BGJ08 was a Google Trends game. Pretty simple and didn't come out as well as I wanted it too. ~50th out of ~90, not awful but not good. People had some problems running the game, these things happen when you use Pygame. I'll look into packing next time round, or maybe move to unity, as that seems to work much better.

Applications

  • Shortcuts

    A suite of Mac OS X services that add functionality to your Mac. These range from opening a web-page in a mobile-style window, allowing for quick Wikipedia searches and hash-tag look-ups without leaving your app or webpage, to organizing your window arrangement.

  • Lightning Timer

    A simple Pygame/Javascript application used to measure the distance to a lightning strike.

Mobile Applications

  • Lightning

    An Android application that let's you time the distance to a lightning strike. Free and compatible will allmost all Android devices (>Froyo).

Arduino

  • Midi Controller

    Use this sketch to turn your Arduino into a Midi controller. Uses up to 16 buttons and has programmable notes and velocities. Designed to be adapted to your project or used as reference for working with Arduino and the Midi interface.
